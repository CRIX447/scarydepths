The VHS Manifest

SCARY DEPTHS is a first-person VR analog horror experience blending retro VHS aesthetics with psychological dread. Set in an abandoned underwater research station, you uncover corrupted tapes revealing a government experiment gone wrong. The year is 1987. Your only equipment: a failing sonar, a portable CRT monitor, a voice recorder catching whispers that shouldn't exist.

Each session generates a unique depth layer — rooms shift, hallways stretch, the same looped broadcast hides messages meant to drive you mad. No combat. Only hiding, environmental puzzles, and managing your sanity by avoiding prolonged exposure to static entities. Every creak and reversed voice clue leads closer to the truth — or to your second death.
